---

## Key Points to Include:

1. ✅ **Clear separation** between free and paid features
2. ✅ **Explicit labeling** - "(Premium Feature)" or "Subscription Required"
3. ✅ **Pricing information** - Make it clear what users pay
4. ✅ **Free trial mention** - "7-day free trial"
5. ✅ **Terms of Use link** - Required by Apple
6. ✅ **No confusion** - Users should immediately understand what's free vs paid

---

## What NOT to Do:

❌ Don't just mention features without indicating they're paid
❌ Don't use vague terms like "Premium" without explaining what it means
❌ Don't hide pricing information
❌ Don't make users guess which features require payment
